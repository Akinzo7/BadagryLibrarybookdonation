GIVE OUT YOUR BOOKS, EMPOWER MINDS, AND MAKE A DIFFERENCE.

Turn your unused books into a source of knowledge for others. By donating to our platform, you can help bridge the gap between those with books to share and those eager to learn.

Donate to Library

Are your favorite books shelved and unused? Would you like to bring a bit of joy to other kids and students? Send your books to one of our collection points. Sending or donating your books is a great way to directly empower children through education. This easy and rewarding task can have a profound and lasting impact on our division library communities!

Package your book into any good box and seal it with tape. Complete our online booking form to arrange a collection from anywhere in Badagry or schedule a drop-off appointment to deliver to a drop-off point near you. After booking, write your number on the box to enable us to process and notify you about the journey and beneficiary of your donated books.
